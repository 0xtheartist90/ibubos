# Editorial Archives and Compact Related Content Design

## Goal

Give the blog and project archive heroes a confident editorial composition while making related content on detail pages clearly secondary.

## Archive Heroes

- Blogs use a text-led left column and a large featured-blog image on the right.
- Projects mirror the composition with the featured image on the left and title/introduction on the right.
- Both heroes use an orange rule, compact labels, and a direct link into the featured item.
- Existing Ibu Bos colors, Mukta Mahee typography, and photography remain unchanged.
- Mobile stacks the content into one clear image-and-text sequence without overlaps.

## Related Content

- Related sections use their own compact horizontal row component rather than archive cards.
- Rows contain a small thumbnail, category, title, and simple arrow indicator.
- Descriptions and large calls to action are omitted.
- Spacing and heading scale are reduced so the section reads as a quiet next step.
- Blog details show three blog rows; project details show two project rows.

## Verification

- Tests confirm the archive heroes link to valid featured items and detail pages keep same-type related content.
- Responsive browser checks cover archive and detail pages at desktop and mobile widths.
- Type checking and a production build confirm all routes compile.
